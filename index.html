Disney 101

Disney • Pixar • Disney Channel • Marvel • Star Wars

Disney 101 is the place you want to come to if you want to learn history on Disney or to get ideas for Disney movie night, Disney costumes, or a Disney playlist. On this website you'll have the chance to explore some of the history behind Disney, as well as my top 10 different Disney movies, characters, and songs. Disney has been around for almost a century and the older Disney gets, the more popular it seems to become. I have a feeling Disney will be around for many more years. I hope you enjoy this website and find your inner Disney-side.
