Top 10 Disney Characters

Here are my top 10 favorite Disney characters. There are over 800 Disney characters (not counting Marvel or Star Wars) alone. It's very hard not to like at least one Disney character since there are so many. And don't forget about your favorite Marvel superhero or your favorite jedi or droid in Star Wars. I hope you find a character that connects with you. If not, you can come up with your own top 10 list of Disney characters.

Enjoy!!!

Disney Characters Place Mat" by George Thomas from Flickr.com is licensed under CC BY-NC-ND 2.0

  1. Winnie the Pooh

    2. "Winnie The Pooh Nursery" by Michelle Vucko from creativecommons.org CC BY-NC-ND 4.0

    Winnie the Pooh was first created in 1926 by A.A. Milne in a children's book titled "Winnie-the-Pooh". Winnie the Pooh lives in Hundred Acre Wood and his best friend is Christopher Robin. Pooh's other friends in Hundred Acre Wood are Piglet, Eeyore, Tigger, Kanga, Roo, and Bunny. Pooh is a lovable bear that would do anything to help his friends or to get honey.

  2. Stitch

    3. "Stitch" by DocChewbacca from Flickr.com CC BY-NC-ND 2.0

    Stitch was first introduced in the movie "Lilo and Stitch" in 2002. Stitch is a blue alien that arrives on Earth and pretends to be dog, but learns how to love with the help of Lilo. Stitch is very mischievous, but he becomes a part of your 'ohana' and you can't help but love him.

  3. Star Butterfly

  4. Rapunzel

    5. "Rapunzel" by Valentina Matusa from creativecommons.org CC BY-NC-ND 4.0

    Rapunzel is a fairytale character that was first written by the Grimm Brothers and has been around for a long time. However she was introduced in Disney during the Disney princess movie "Tangled" in 2010. Rapunzel is a very curious and determined princess and she's willing to step outside her comfort zone to chase down her dreams.

  5. Thor

    6. "Thor The Dark World" by The_JIFF from creativecommons.org CC BY 2.0

    Thor is the famous god of thunder in Norse mythology. He's also the son of Odin, and the half-brother of Loki, the god of mischief and trickery. Thor first appeared in MCU (Marvel Cinematic Universe) during his own movie "Thor" in 2011. Thor uses his famous hammer to protect Asgard and protects Earth as a member of the Avengers with the aid of his God of Thunder abilities, of course.

  6. Maleficent

  7. Olaf

    8. "frozen" by Jorge Figueroa from Flickr.com CC BY 2.0

    Olaf is a snowman from the Disney movie "Frozen". He was created by Elsa's power in Arendelle and is the best friend of Anna and Elsa. He loves all things summer and even sings a song about how much he likes summer. One of his famous lines is, "Hi I'm Olaf and I like warm hugs!"

  8. Genie

    9. "disney-aladdin-genie-03" by Maurina Rara from Flickr.com CC BY 2.0

    The Genie is a blue wish-granting figure featured in the movie "Aladdin" in the year of 1992. Genie has the power to grant his master three wishes, however Genie's only wish is to be set free. Genie is a lovable and hilarious character, you can't help but just laugh at his funny antics or smile at his kindess as a friend. By the way, don't worry too much about the Genie's wish because it eventually comes true and [Spoiler Alert] just in case you haven't seen "Aladdin".

  9. Cheshire Cat

    10. "cheshire-cat-4" by Rikki's Refuge from Flickr.com CC BY 2.0

    The Cheshire Cat is a cat that originally appeared in "Aliceâ€™s Adventures in Wonderland" written by Lewis Carroll in 1865. The cheshire cat has striped, lines of fur and he has the ability to disappear and reappear. Sometimes it's body disappears, but leaves his smile behind. The cheshire cat isn't as mad as the Mad Hatter, but his witty metaphors are very close to being on a mad scale.

  10. Jack Jack

    11. "Jack Jack" by Smiley Man with a Hat from Flickr.com CC BY-NC-ND 2.0

    Jack Jack is the youngest child of Mr. Incredible (Bob Parr) and Elsatagirl (Helen Parr) , and is also the baby sibling to Violet and Dash Parr. He has a number of powers including polymorphing, teleportation, levitation, laser eyes, self-duplication, telekinesis, and many more super powers. Jack Jack may have the most powers in the Incredible (Parr) family, but he's the cutest family member by far.
