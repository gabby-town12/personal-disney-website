Top 10 Disney Channel Shows

There are over [enter number] Disney Channel tv shows and more in the making. Here are my top 10 favorite Disney shows of all time. My shows range between the years of 2000 to 2022. A couple of these tv shows have also won Emmys. If you need a trailer for any tv show just click on the photo of the show. I hope you find a Disney Channel tv show that's perfect for your next binge-watching session.

Happy Watching!!!

"Disney Logo (Photo)" by Shakir Superville from creativecommons.org CC BY 2.0

  1. Cinderella (2014)

    2. "Become Enchanted Again" by BagoGames from Flickr.com CC BY 2.0

    This live-action Disney classic tells the story of a girl named Ella that is forced to live with her wicked stepmother and evil stepsisters after the death of her father. One day the prince sends an invitation to a ball and Cinderella is forbidden to attend the ball and is not allowed to see the handsome stranger she met in the woods, but with the help of a little magic and glass slippers, she'll find her happily ever after. (Warning: You might get teary-eyed)

  2. Tangled (2010)

    3. "Tangled gold logo" by Carniolus from Commons.Wikipedia.org Public Domain Mark 1.0

    Princess Rapunzel has been locked up in a tower since the day she was kidnapped by Mother Gothel. Rapunzel possesses magical 70-feet long hair that gives eternal youth and must be kept safe. As her 18th birthday nears, she receives help from Flynn Rider and makes Flynn take her to see the floating lanterns and live out her dream. (Warning: You may need tissues)

  3. The Little Mermaid (1989)

    4. "The Little Mermaid" by Jørgen Håland from Flickr.com CC BY 2.0

    The Little Mermaid is a tail about a 16 year-old mermaid named Ariel. Ariel's father, King Triton, has forbidden her from going to the surface, but on one her secret visits she saves a human, Prince Eric, from drowning and falls in love with him. Wanting to be with her human love Ariel strikes a deal with the sea witch, Ursuala, but is unaware of Ursula's true intentions.

  4. Beauty and the Beast (1991)

    5. "Beauty and the Beast" by Catherine Chen from Flickr.com CC BY-NC 2.0

    Beauty and the Beast tells the story of a selfish prince who is forced to live his life as a beast. The only way for the prince to break the spell is for him to learn to how to love. One night, a girl named Belle enters his palace in search of her imrpisoned father, Maurice. Belle takes the place of her father in prison and lives in the palace with the Beast. With the help of the enchanted servants, she develops a relationship with the beast that may just break the spell.

  5. Turning Red (2022)

    6. "Beauty and the Beast" by Catherine Chen from Flickr.com CC BY-NC 2.0

    Beauty and the Beast tells the story of a selfish prince who is forced to live his life as a beast. The only way for the prince to break the spell is for him to learn to how to love.   6. High School Musical 3 (2008)

    7. "High School Musical 3 Logo" by Escudero from Commons.Wikipedia.org Public Domain Mark 1.0

    High School Musical is the third film in the High School Musical franchise. It's senior year for the wildcats and they have to overcome the idea of going to college and going their seperate ways after high school. They stage a Spring Musical to help them reveal their hopes and fears of the near future.

  7. The Incredibles (2004)

    8. "The Incredibles logo" by Mr White from Commons.Wikipedia.org Public Domain Mark 1.0

    The Incredibles centers on a family full of superheroes. Mr. Incredible (aka. Bob Parr) and Elstagirl (aka. Helen Parr) used to fight crime and save the city, but 15 years later they're forced to live normal lives with their three super children, Violet, Dash, and Jack Jack. Bob misses those old days as Mr. Incredible and is given the chance to be a superhero again when he's summoned to a private island to battle an out of control robot. However, Mr. Incredible finds himself in trouble and needs help from the rest of the Incredible family to save the world.

  8. Another Cinderella Story (2008)

    9. "Another Cinderella Story" by Everyone add my second account! ((Bri)) from Flickr.com CC BY 2.0

    Another Cinderella Story is a about a talented dancer named Mary that is forced to live with her evil Stepmother and stepsisters and work for them. When Mary goes the Masquerade Ball she dances with popstar, Joey Parker, and they have connection. But Mary has to return home to make her curfew and the only thing Joey knows about Mary is the playlist on her zune. Joey then goes on a search to find the mystery girl he fell in love with.

  9. Guardians of the Galaxy Vol.2 (2017)

    10. "New GOTG Vol. 2 Poster!" by AntMan3001 from Flickr.com CC BY-SA 2.0

    Guardians of the Galaxy Vol.2 is the second installment in the Marvel Guardians of the Galaxy flim franchise. In the second film the Guardians are now seen as heros and are tasked with a mission to protect valuable batteries. But when Rocket steals some they go on the run and meet Peter Quill's (aka Star Lord) father. They journey to his father's home planet to learn more about who he is; however, Peter's father isn't telling the the Guardians the truth about everything.

  10. Up (2009)

    11. "up-creative-movie-posters" by jdxyw from Flickr.com CC BY-SA 2.0

    Up is a movie focusing on an old man named Mr. Fredricksen who goes on a journey to Paradise Falls in South America. He ties thousands of balloons to his house and on his journey he encounters a few unexpected guests. Such as Russel, a boy scout; Kevin, a strange bird; and Dug, a talking dog.

  11. Venom (2018)

    12. "VENOM - 11 x17 Original Promo Movie Poster 2018 Tom Hardy Marvel Cinemark" by Bernice Turner from Flickr.com CC0 1.0

    Venom is a Marvel movie about a journalist named Eddie Brock. After Eddie has a failed interview with Carlton Drake, the founder of Life Foundation, Eddie is fired. He returns to Life Foundation to investigate one of their experiments and merges with a parisitic alien symbiote called Venom. Venom's purpose is to bring back more symbiotes to Earth to find hosts, but he starts to take a liking of Earth and Venom and Eddie find a way to stop the other symbiotes from coming to Earth.
